Our Meeting

      Gabar Ewen, Huy Pham, Justin Cruz

      A Meeting Management API designed to allow Engineers to quickly develop conferencing solutions using the WebRTC API.

      This idea was inspired by the constant need of a reliable video conferencing application as an alternative to Google Hangout for Hack Reactor, a Software Engineering School. By relying on current technologies such as WebRTC, Socket.io, Node.js and Express, the team developed an API service to provide a conferencing solution. Further, the service was designed to be easily integrated into any Learning Management System.
